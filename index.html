Levels of Ulead Program

Youth Influencer

This will be Level 1 of this internship program. Youth Influencers works on marketing Unschool Courses and gaining traction online, building operations and getting matches, and advertising on different social media platforms. Get Promoted to the next level on successful completion of the given tasks.

Youth Leader

This will be Level 2 of this internship program. Youth Leaders lead the revolution by building and mentoring a team and and ace at leadership skills and operations. They represent Unschool at events and build marketing and operations strategies.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Ulead?
    • Ulead is a flagship internship programme in the domain of sales and marketing powered by unschool
  • Should we pay for this internship?
    • No, you don't have to pay any amount for this internship program
  • Who can apply ?
    • Students in higher education, under graduation and post-graduation and having Strong leadership and interpersonal skills, can apply. Preferred experience in college fests, societies or clubs.
  • How long is this internship?
    • The tenure of this internship will be one month.
  • What are the working hours?
    • The working hours are flexible. But interns should spare an hour in the evening.
  • How much stipend will I get ?
    • In this internship programme we are offering Performance-Based incentives and benefits. Well-performing interns will also get promotions.
  • What is the application process?
    • Interested students can fill the application form on the Ulead website with the required credentials.
  • What happens after we apply?
    • The applicants will receive a telephonic interview call for the selection process.
